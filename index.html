SF Crime: Two Decades of Data

This project explores long-term patterns in crime across San Francisco, uncovering how different offenses evolve over time and space. Through interactive visualizations, you can explore when crimes happen, how they relate, and where they concentrate across the city.

📊 Crime Correlations

This visualization compares pairs of crime types to reveal relationships between them. Each point represents an hour in the week, helping identify whether certain crimes tend to increase or decrease together.

Explore how crime fluctuates throughout the week. This interactive visualization allows you to identify peak hours and understand how different crime categories behave across time.
